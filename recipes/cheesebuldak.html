Cheese Buldak Recipe

About Cheese Buldak

Buldak is a Korean dish which translates to fire chicken. It became popular in South Korea during 2004, as the country's long term recession and economic downturn caused the local population to seek out spicy food in order to relieve stress.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb boneless chicken thigh
  • 1/4 small cabbage
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1/2 onion
  • mozzarella cheese

Buldak Sauce

  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 3 tbsp gochugaru
  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Add all the buldak sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Add diced chicken, cabbage, ginger, garlic and onions in a bowl.
  3. Pour the buldak sauce into the bowl and mix well.
  4. Let it marinate at room temperature for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Pan fry the chicken in a cast iron skillet/oven safe pan over high heat for 10 minutes or until the chicken cooks. Stirring occasionally.
  6. Add cheese on top and broil for 3-5 minutes in the oven. Alternatively, use a kitchen torch. Leave until cheese fully melts.
